Masai Mara National Reserve

The Masai Mara National Reserve is renowned for its abundant wildlife, including the Big Five (lion, leopard, elephant, buffalo, and rhinoceros). Safari drives in this reserve offer unforgettable experiences amidst the vast savannah landscapes.

Amboseli National Park

Amboseli National Park is famous for its stunning views of Mount Kilimanjaro and large herds of elephants. Safari tours here provide opportunities to witness diverse wildlife against the backdrop of Africa's highest peak.

Samburu National Reserve

National Reserve is home to unique wildlife species such as the reticulated giraffe, Grevy's zebra, and Somali ostrich. Safaris in this reserve offer a chance to explore the semi-arid landscapes and spot rare wildlife.

Lake Nakuru National Park

Nakuru National Park is renowned for its flamingos and diverse birdlife. Safari tours here also provide opportunities to see rhinos, lions, and other wildlife species in their natural habitat.

Charge per day: $75

Tsavo East and West National Parks

Tsavo East and West National Parks are among the largest wildlife reserves in Kenya, known for their vast landscapes and diverse wildlife. Safari adventures here offer encounters with elephants, lions, and other iconic African animals.
