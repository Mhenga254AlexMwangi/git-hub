Explore Kenya's Beautiful Beaches

Mombasa Beach

along the Indian Ocean coastline, Mombasa offers pristine beaches with golden sands and crystal-clear waters. Enjoy water sports, explore historic sites, and indulge in delicious coastal cuisine. Price per day: $50.

Lamu Beach

to Lamu Island for a tranquil beach getaway. With its untouched beauty and rich Swahili culture, Lamu offers a serene atmosphere and opportunities for dhow sailing, snorkeling, and dolphin watching. Price per day: $60.

Diani Beach

Beach is renowned for its powdery white sands and turquoise waters. Experience luxury resorts, vibrant nightlife, and water-based activities like kite surfing and scuba diving. Price per day: $70.

Watamu Beach

between pristine coral reefs, Watamu Beach is a paradise for nature lovers. Explore the Watamu Marine National Park, relax on secluded shores, and witness spectacular marine life. Price per day: $65.

Malindi Beach

picturesque beaches, ancient ruins, and vibrant markets. Dive into the colorful underwater world, visit historical landmarks, and savor fresh seafood delicacies. Price per day: $55.
